About Techno Fun 2K24

Techno Fun 2K24 is a vibrant tech festival hosted by the Computer Science Department of The Shaikh Ayaz University, Shikarpur. This annual event brings together technology enthusiasts, students, and professionals from various fields to engage in competitions, exhibitions, and fun activities. Our goal is to create an engaging platform for innovation, learning, and networking, all while celebrating the spirit of technology and creativity.

Join us on 12th and 13th November 2024, for a series of events including programming challenges, project showcases, gaming competitions, and a special musical night.
